# ReactCam Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 5, 2026

ReactCam ("we", "the app") is designed with privacy in mind.

## Data We Access
- **Camera** — used locally on your device for the live AI segmentation overlay and recording. Camera frames are never transmitted, stored remotely, or shared.
- **Microphone** — used locally during recording only. Audio is never transmitted or stored remotely.
- **Photo Library** — used only to save videos you record and to let you pick videos for playback. We do not read, analyze, or upload your library.

## Data We Collect
**None.** ReactCam does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There is no account system, no analytics SDK, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

## In-App Purchases
Purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We never see your payment information.

## Children's Privacy
ReactCam is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
